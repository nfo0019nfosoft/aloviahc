About Laser Hair Removal

The removal of unwanted body hair is often part of our daily routines. Shaving, waxing, plucking, and other methods are all commonplace. For people who no longer want these activities to be part of their everyday life, or who have unwanted hair in places it may be difficult to get rid of, laser hair removal offers a way to see long lasting and frequently permanent results.

How Does Laser Hair Removal Work?

Laser hair removal uses concentrated beams of light to prevent hair follicles from growing hair. Like many other laser procedures, it relies on melanin. During treatment, the laser’s light beams are absorbed by the pigment in your hair. It targets the hair follicles via the melanin produced in your hair.

Once it reaches the hair follicle, the light energy is converted into heat where it damages the follicles to inhibit future hair growth. Laser removal requires multiple sessions to target your hair at all stages of growth. As an example, at your first session, only a portion of hair follicles are treated because hair follicles can lay dormant for weeks. Hair follicles alternate on which produce hair and which do not. This explains why multiple sessions are required for the best results.

Benefits Of Hair Removal

Laser hair removal comes with many notable benefits. Not only can it provide lasting hair removal, but it also can save you money and time in the long run.

Benefits of laser hair removal can include:

  • Removal of unwanted hair
  • Long lasting and often permanent
  • Discontinuation of shaving and waxing
  • No longer needing to buy razors or other hair removal products
  • Prevents ingrown hairs
  • Cessation of side effects from shaving like razor bumps
  • Low risk procedure
  • Hypoallergenic
  • Procedure usually takes no more than an hour
  • Scarring extremely rare
  • Benefits in transgender transition and can alleviate gender dysphoria for some patients (especially male to female)
  • Little to no pain with long lasting results

The Procedure:

You usually do not find treatment overly painful. You may feel some pinching and heat, but nothing too painful. Depending on the size of the treatment area and the number of areas, laser hair removal can take anywhere from 20 minutes to one hour or more.

Aftercare:

After laser hair removal at ALOVIA our expert doctor will give you instructions on how to care for your skin to promote the best results. Instructions may include:

Avoid exercise, hot showers, saunas, or anything that may cause you to sweat or get hot for around 24 hours after treatment

Do not go out in the sun and wear sunscreen

Gently exfoliate after about five days

Attend your next sessions

At Home vs. Professional Laser Hair Removal

A variety of at home laser removal devices and kits are available. They are initially tempting because it allows you to do it yourself at home. Are these devices effective? Are they safe?

The answer to both of these questions is no. Lasers sold in stores for hair removal often are not as strong or customizable as professional lasers. While they may come with a few settings, every person receives the same treatment. With professional laser hair removal, the laser settings are personalized to fit you. This reduces the risks of burns and promotes the best results.

Also, at-home laser devices run the risk of burns or permanent skin damage. Master estheticians are trained in laser procedures, including hair removal. Their skill reduces the chances of burns or other risks.

How Many Sessions Will I Need? +

It works best on darker hair. Blonde hair may need special treatment.
