FAT LOSS

Most people have heard of FAT FREEZING or the science of freezing fat cells with a device that shoots super-cooled air across areas of stubborn fat. The cool air kills the fat cells, destroying them for good.

FAT COOLING Elite is the more advanced version featuring applicators that cover more target areas at once, as well as being twice as effective during the treatment.

Once the targeted fat cells are dead, your body metabolizes them naturally over the next few weeks. The treated areas become slimmer and slimmer before your very eyes.

During the treatment, you lie back and relax as the FAT COOLING device works its freezing magic. Afterward, the technician performs a gentle warming massage and you can hop up and go on with your day.

Best for: Thigh, abdomen, and flank.
