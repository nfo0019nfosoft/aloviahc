MICROBLADING

Microblading has been described as the next big thing in the beauty industry and one of the most dominant techniques in the field of semi-permanent cosmetics and treatments. It offers one of the most natural-looking and defined eyebrow treatments.

Microblading is a semi-permanent make-up procedure that involves manually creating lines that mimic your natural brow hair. The golden ratio is used to create the most natural brow hair appearance and brow shape that fits your face 100% accurately

Features and benefits of microblading

  • One less thing to think about when applying your make-up
  • Ideal for transforming patchy eyebrows
  • Waterproof once healed
  • Ideal for those who have lost their eyebrows due to illness, alopecia or chemotherapy

OMBRE BROWS

The ombre brow technique is ideal for clients who prefer a fuller brow. The tails of the brow are darker and fade naturally, giving a perfect ombre effect. The results are waterproof, smudge-proof and look completely natural.

The technique uses a small machine to place extremely tiny dots of pigment into the skin, creating a soft-shaded brow pencil look. Ombre shading provides 1 to 3 years of low maintenance for your brows.

Features and benefits of ombre brows

  • Ideal option for oily or sensitive skin, unlike microblading
  • Requires fewer touch-up appointments
  • Gives the appearance of the fuller, more defined brows often seen on Instagram

LIP BLUSH

Lip blush or satin lips can enhance the colour and shape of your lips. The technique deposits coloured ink into your lips and along your lip line to improve their overall appearance. The colour is subtle to give natural-looking results.

Lip blush is one of the most popular lip treatments available to enhance your lip size and symmetry, without the need for dermal fillers. If you already have dermal fillers, please wait 3 weeks after your filler procedure and ensure your lips are fully settled before attending your appointment.

Features and benefits of lip blush

  • Neutralises lip pigment
  • Improve overall lip symmetry
  • A good alternative to fillers
  • Brighter fuller smile
  • Can help boost your confidence
